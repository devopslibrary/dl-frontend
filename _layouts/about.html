What is the DevOps Library?

The DevOps Library is a free, online learning resource for aspiring DevOps Engineers, System Admins, Developers, Site Reliability Engineers, Automation Engineers, and anyone else who has an interest in DevOps. We strive to create not only the highest quality videos, but to always remain a free resource for the DevOps community.

Who is the DevOps Library?

The DevOps Library was founded in early 2015, by Ken Erwin & Samantha Ventura.
